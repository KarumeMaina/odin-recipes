Pasta Moussaka Recipe

Description:

Moussaka is a layered oven casserole dish made with vegetables and meat.
The most well-known version of moussaka is made with layers of eggplant slices,
cheese, and a meat sauce, topped with a thick béchamel sauce; however, other
favorites call for potatoes, zucchini, or a combination of vegetables. More recently,
a meatless (vegetarian) version has also become widely available.

Until the early 1900s, moussaka was a more simple dish, using just vegetables and
meats. The addition of béchamel sauce is attributed to Nikos Tselementes, a Greek
chef who trained in France, and this newer version quickly became a favorite in
Greece.
